About the company

The Comedy Addict LLC is a US-registered limited liability company building media products for comedy fans. We're independent, comedian-friendly, and small enough to ship things we'd actually want to use ourselves.

Our flagship product, Chuckle, is launching on iOS. More projects are in the works — newsletters, live shows, and short-form video formats that put working comedians first.
